Eight Layers

FMA Instructor Development Assessment

Leadership in Filipino Martial Arts isn't something you perform or learn in a weekend course. It emerges naturally when you strengthen foundational competencies across eight critical layers.

The Eight Foundational Layers:

  1. Technical Skill & Fundamentals
  2. Teaching Ability
  3. Maturity & Judgment
  4. Program Identity & Mission
  5. Curriculum & Operations
  6. Community & Culture (Stewardship)
  7. Student Responsibility & Alignment
  8. Leadership (Emergent)

What to Expect:

  • 48 questions across all eight layers
  • Takes approximately 10-15 minutes
  • Honest self-reflection required
  • Instant personalized results
  • Actionable development paths

Privacy: This assessment runs entirely in your browser. No data is collected or stored.
